About this site:

This site is created by R.L.Irabon

This site aims to bridge communication gaps between individuals who speak different languages.With user-friendly interface and accurate translations, also strives to breakdown language barriers and promote cross-cultural communications.
This sites mission is to empower people to connect and understand each other better, no matter where they are in the world.

I would like to acknowledge the inspiration I gained from Opensource-coding while working on this Language Translator. Their innovative approach and creative solutions greatly influenced my work and helped me to achieve my goals for this website.
